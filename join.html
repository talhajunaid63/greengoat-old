We're looking for individuals with a strong sense of purpose and the brainpower to back it all up:

Interns
Depending on the season, interns manage market development, specific research, and fundraising. Although unpaid, this position is an excellent gateway to architecture, construction management, and of course environmental consulting. Most assignments are three months in duration with an option on both sides to renew.

Financial Officer
Candidate needs to be able to run the books, pay the bills, forecast revenue, and do this without stealing us blind. Demonstrated familiarity with activity-based costing and cost drivers in the construction industry is important.

Regulatory Officer
Candidate will be familiar with the effect of regulatory models and building code on the evolution of this industry. Federal and local trends are beginning to move quickly with regards to conservation, and they interact with design solutions as well as press relations. An equal sensitivity to thoroughness and alacrity is important.

Interested parties should attach a resume to an email describing how they might add momentum to greenGoat's foothold in enviro-development to thegoat [at] greengoat.org
